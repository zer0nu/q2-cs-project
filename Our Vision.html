So what's Our Vision?

We want to create our app, GetHelp. This app could be thought of as a social media but aimed specifically for mental health issues. Not only will we have consultation with other people, but with trained profressionals that we will hire.

The App will have the following:

Personal Chats to Our Therapists

This will be the main focus of our app, connecting users and our professionals easier than ever. We plan not to just limit our app to messaging, but we could also have voicechats or videochats that could be paid (if the therapist wishes so). This accessibility to trained therapists is something we think is groundbreaking, because with just the touch of a finger, we could be reducing depression and suicide cases by huge numbers.

Group Chats with other users

We will also have group chats like other social media apps, this is to really help meet people with the same problems as you and of course make friends on our platform.

Algorithm for meeting with new people

Lastly, we plan to have an "Omegle" kind of meeting system, where people can input the kind of problems or experiences they might be going through, and be matched up people of a similar demographic through our AI to talk and help each other get through similar tought times. You can of course friend these people and talk even more or add them to groupchats.

Our Predicted Benefits

We want to build a world where we can finally normalize the fact that mental health is an issue to be taken with serious demeanor. We believe that with our app we can not only help a huge demographic of people but make them happier in life, not to mention meeting a few friends along the way. Laslty, we predict that if we are successful enough with our launch, we could live in a world where as a therapist or doctor, it is normal to have a GetHelp account where people can consult you which could help you in building a career from our simple app.
