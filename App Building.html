Recommended Page

As stated in Our Vision page, we want to create a platform to connect users and profressionals easier than ever. We will have a dedicated part of the app which let's you seek out therapists and view their ratings by other users.

Personal Chats to Profressionals

Once you select an available therapist, you may start chatting them. Again, we will not limit our app only to chatting but also include possible voice calls or video calls which can be paid or free depending on the choice of the therapist.

Personalized Group Chats

And like any other traditional social media app, we will have group Chats where you can chat friends or anonymous users who may be facing similar problems and support each other. Video Chats and Voice Chats will of course be free here.
